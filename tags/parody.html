    Every Rose Has Its … Prickle

    Michael Lockhart

    2018-08-19 21:40

    You may be aware: roses don't have thorns, they have prickles. This is a fact that the band Poison were either unaware of, or (wisely) chose to overlook in their song Every Rose Has Its Thorn.

    Imagine if they hadn't overlooked it:

    Every rose has its prickle

    Just like every night, we gotta trickle

    Just like every cowboy has a soft place to tickle

    Every rose has its prickle.
